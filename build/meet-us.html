Do you fit in?

36Squared welcomes “Partners” whose business or activities are not disruptive to other “Partners” in the building. Generally, that means if your business involves loud music, sawing, chemicals, machinery, pounding, or flamethrowers, you’re not going to make a good neighbor. We have a wide variety of current “Partner” uses from professional and office services, light product and package assembly, web startups, artistic endeavors.
